About Savannah

Savannah was born during a blizzard and never gets cold. She was so independent as a baby that she never cried when I handed her to another person. She was a happy baby who tried to talk to everyone. We used to walk around Philadelphia making friends wherever we went. Now she is a 3.5 and loving life in the suburbs. She has lots of friends at school. Her pretend play is hilarious and creative.
